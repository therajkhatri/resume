Hydropower O&M

Hydropower Operation and Maintenance (O&M) involves the daily running, preventive maintenance, and repairs of hydroelectric plants to ensure maximum efficiency, safety, and longevity (30-35+ years).

Saftey and Compliance

Safety and compliance constitute the ongoing process of adhering to legal standards, regulations (such as OSHA), and internal policies designed to protect worker health and prevent workplace accidents.

Fault Troubleshooting

Fault troubleshooting is a systematic, logical process for identifying and resolving the root cause of a failure in a system to restore normal operation. It involves a 6-point approach: defining the problem, gathering data, analyzing symptoms, identifying the fault, implementing a solution, and testing/documenting the repair.

Team Management

Team management skills are the ability to organize, lead, and support a group of people to achieve shared goals efficiently, combining soft skills like communication and emotional intelligence with strategic planning. Key skills include delegation, conflict resolution, goal setting (using SMART criteria), and maintaining a positive, trusting environment that boosts productivity.

Documentation

Documentation skills are the ability to create, manage, and maintain accurate, organized, and clear records, reports, or technical documents. Essential in professions like healthcare and IT, these skills involve clear communication, attention to detail, and proficiency in tools to ensure records are accessible and compliant.
